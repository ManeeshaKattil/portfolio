KAHM UNITY WOMEN'S COLLEGE MANJERI

KAHM Unity Women's College was established in 1991 and is run by Muslim Educational and Cultural Association (MECA), a registered society. Started as a First Grade Aided College affiliated to the University of Calicut, it is the first multi disciplinary (Arts, Science & Commerce) women's college in Malappuram District. It has also the unique distinction of being the first institution of this kind run by the Muslim Community in Kerala.
